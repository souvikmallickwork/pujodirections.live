Introducing Pujo Gpt

Pujo GPT is an AI-powered chatbot designed to enhance your Durga Pujo experience. Built using OpenAI and LLaMA technologies, it provides real-time answers about Pujo traditions, events, and pandal locations. Whether you’re curious about rituals, need recommendations, or want local insights, Pujo GPT is your personal guide. Users can also contribute their own experiences to continuously improve the chatbot’s knowledge, making it smarter and more helpful with each interaction.
