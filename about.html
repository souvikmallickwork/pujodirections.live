About Pujo Directions

Pujo Directions is a comprehensive app that allows users to explore Durga Pujo pandals, get real-time updates, and connect with the Pujo-loving community. Our goal is to make the Durga Pujo experience accessible, safe, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to keep the tradition of Pujo exploration alive by blending technology with age-old customs, providing an authentic yet modern experience for all.

Features at a Glance:

  • Interactive Maps
  • Emergency Helplines
  • Live Feed of Pujo Events
  • Pujo GPT: Your AI Guide
